who is clive cooper

Clive Cooper is a year-old male living in Wellington, New Zealand. Clive graduated from Massey University in 2021 with a Bachelor of Creative Media Production (specialisation in Web Dev) and a Minor in Music Technology. He is passionate about Interactive Design and has been working on web projects for more than 10 years. Along the way, Clive has picked up an interest in Music Production and has been learning the craft in his spare time, releasing two albums on Bandcamp.
